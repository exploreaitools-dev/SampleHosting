What Our Clients Ask

How quickly do you respond?

We respond to all enquiries within one business day. For active clients on a managed support plan, our team is available 24/7 — with most urgent responses acknowledged within 2 hours.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely. We work with businesses of all sizes — from 5-person start-ups to large enterprise teams. Every engagement is scoped and priced to match your size and budget, not a one-size-fits-all package.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes, completely. Our discovery call is a genuine no-obligation conversation — no pitch, no pressure. We'll give you honest advice on whether and how we can help, even if that means pointing you elsewhere.

Can you work with our existing IT team?

Yes — many of our engagements are collaborative. We can augment your in-house team, provide specialist expertise, or act as a strategic advisor working alongside your existing staff without disruption.

How do you price your services?

We use transparent project-based or retainer pricing. Every proposal is fully itemised so you know exactly what you're paying for — no hidden fees, no scope creep surprises. We'll walk you through every line.

Do you offer ongoing IT support after project completion?

Yes. Most clients continue with a managed support retainer after delivery. We proactively monitor, maintain, and optimise your systems — catching issues before they impact your business or your team.

Can your solutions scale as our business grows?

That's exactly how we design them. Every solution we deliver is architected for scalability — whether you're doubling headcount, expanding into new markets, or adding new product lines down the line.

How experienced and what certifications does your team hold?

Our team brings 15+ years of combined IT consulting experience. Specialists hold certifications across AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, CISSP, CompTIA Security+, and Microsoft — with credentials continuously updated as the industry evolves.

How do you measure the success of a project?

We agree on clear success metrics at the start of every engagement — typically uptime targets, cost savings, deployment speed, or user adoption rates. You'll receive regular progress reports and a final outcome summary tied to those agreed goals.

How do you ensure our company data remains secure?

Security is built in from day one, not bolted on at the end. We follow ISO 27001, NIST, and SOC 2 principles, enforce least-privilege access controls, and conduct a post-project security review on every engagement we complete.

What is your typical response time (SLA) for critical issues?

For managed service clients, our SLA for critical (P1) issues is a 1-hour acknowledgement with a 4-hour resolution target. We offer tiered SLA packages so the coverage always matches the operational criticality of your business.

What makes your company different from other IT consultants?

Most IT consultants disappear after delivery. We don't. Our model is built on long-term partnership — outcome-driven engagements, fully transparent pricing, and proactive support that keeps your technology evolving. A 98% client retention rate and 15+ years in the field means clients come back because the results are genuinely there.
