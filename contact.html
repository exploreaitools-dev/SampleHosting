Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond?

We respond to all enquiries within one business day. For urgent IT issues, active clients have 24/7 support access.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely. We work with businesses of all sizes — from 5-person startups to large enterprise teams. We tailor every engagement to your scale.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes, completely. Our discovery call is a genuine no-obligation conversation. No pitch, no pressure — just honest advice.

What countries do you operate in?

We serve clients across India, USA, UK, Australia, Canada, and the UAE — with local teams in each region.

Can you work with our existing IT team?

Yes — many engagements are collaborative. We can augment your team, provide specialist support, or act as a strategic partner alongside in-house staff.

How do you price your services?

We use transparent project-based or retainer pricing. All proposals are fully itemised — no hidden fees, no surprises.
